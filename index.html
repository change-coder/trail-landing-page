Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the different modes of payment available?

    You can use any one of the following payment methods:

    • Credit card of all leading Banks
    • Debit of all leading banks
    • Net Banking
    • UPI Payments and E-Wallets

  • Are there any hidden charges while making payment?

    There are absolutely no hidden charges. You pay only the amount that you see in your order summary page. In some cases, if the courier delivery person asks for any extra charges then kindly contact us.

  • What are the shipping charges?

    When you place any order of less than 500gm then a shipping charge of Rs.50 gets added to your bill.
    When you place any order between 501gm to 999gm then a shipping charge of Rs.75 gets added to your bill.
    The shipping charges are waived off when you place an order of above Rs.1000.

  • Is it necessary to have an account to place an order on Homoeomeds?

    Yes, it is necessarily have an account with us to place an order. It is highly recommended you to create a Homoeomeds account. This will allow you to have a track record with us regarding all your orders.

  • How do I know my order has been confirmed?

    Once your order is placed and confirmed, you will see a confirmation page displaying a unique Order ID, shipping address with the estimated date of delivery. In addition, you will receive an SMS & email confirming your order details.
